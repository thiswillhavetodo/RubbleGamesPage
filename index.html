Rubble Games is an indie games studio based in Sheffield, UK.

We make HTML5 games using the Phaser game engine.

Our Projects:

Zombie Cannon Attack! is a zombie themed endless runner.

Fire test zombies from your cannon and help them infect the local populace to create your very own rampaging zombie horde. Gather data and use it to upgrade your zombies and create the perfect undead army.

Zombie Cannon Attack! is available now to download for FREE from the Google Play Store.
You can also try the demo version in your browser here, or find out more in our blog.

Super Endless Kingdom is a fast-paced retro arcade shoot 'em up with RPG and resource management elements.

Battle against the horrors of the badlands and win the treasure you need to restore your once-great Kingdom to it's former glory while defending your borders from jealous neighbours.

Super Endless Kingdom is in open beta testing. It is fully playable, but it's likely there will be changes before the final release.
Feedback is always welcome, you can play for FREE on Kongregate or play and view the trailer here.
You can also find out more in our blog.
