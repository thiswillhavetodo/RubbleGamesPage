PRIVACY POLICY

This privacy policy applies to the app version of Idle Space Soldier and is correct as of August 2024. It is subject to change from time to time, and we will inform you of changes through the google play store page, discord channel and other methods where available and appropriate.

Rubble Games does not currently collect or store any of your personal information. However, our ad partners will collect and store the following infomation via the app:

User's approximate location, personal identifiers (but NOT name, email, phone number, address, gender, race etc), purchase history, page views while watching ads, app usage times, diagnostics and device information.

This information is collected fo the purposes of advertising or marketing, analytics, app functionality, or fraud prevention, security, and compliance.

Our current ad partners are Unity Ads and Unity Levelplay.
